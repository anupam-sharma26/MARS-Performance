# MARS-Performance
This dashboard tracks influencer campaign performance, showing ad counts, reach, and revenue. It highlights app contribution, influencer rankings, and campaign details, revealing that most reach and revenue come from closed ads and top-performing influencers.




This MARS Influencer Performance Report dashboard tracks advertising campaign performance across influencers, apps, and platforms.

🔢 Key KPIs (Top Section)

Open Ads (69): Ads currently active or not yet completed.

Closed Ads (476): Completed campaigns.

Total Ads (545): Combined count of all campaigns.

Total Reach (4.30M): Total audience reached across all ads.

Amount (372.03K): Total spend or revenue generated from campaigns.

📋 App Performance Table

Breaks down performance by application:

Code 71: Lower volume (143 ads) but moderate reach (936K).

Coupon Rush: Dominates performance with 402 ads and ~3.36M reach.

Includes metrics for open ads, closed ads, total ads, reach, and amount.

🥧 Distribution Insights

Total Ads by App: Coupon Rush contributes ~73.7% of total ads, showing strong dominance over Code 71.

Total Amount by Status: ~92% of revenue comes from closed ads, confirming that completed campaigns drive financial results.

The dashboard highlights:
